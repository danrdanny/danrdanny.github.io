Around 50% of individuals with a suspected genetic disorder remain undiagnosed after a complete clinical evaluation, which often takes years to complete. Variant,s of unknown significance complicate the process and lead to uncertainty. We believe this burden on patients and families is simply too high.

In the Miller Lab, our goals are twofold: to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of genetic testing, and to better understand the causes of human disease through the identification of novel disease-causing mechanisms.
